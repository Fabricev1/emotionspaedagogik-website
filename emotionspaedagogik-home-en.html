Our approach

About the institute and Katrin Michl

A recognised institute for professional emotional competence

The Institut für Emotionspädagogik is a professional training institute specialising in emotional competence for people in responsibility roles.

We provide structured, practice-oriented education for leaders, educators, and professionals who work under pressure and guide others — with recognised training paths up to Emotional Pedagogue (IHK).

Our programmes focus on applied competence, not self-help — helping leaders remain calm, communicate clearly, and act responsibly in complex human situations.

What defines the institute

  • Structured methodology in Emotional Pedagogy
  • Recognised training paths
  • Certified further education with a formal professional framework
