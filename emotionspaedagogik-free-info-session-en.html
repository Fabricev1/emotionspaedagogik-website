Who it's for (and not for)

Perfect if…

✓

You carry emotional responsibility for others in your work — e.g. school leadership, teaching, social and health professions, HR, team leads, or self-employed experts.

✓

You want recognised, practical professional development — with clarity on how the IHK path fits your role.

✓

You prefer structured, non-therapeutic tools that you can explain to colleagues, supervisors, or HR.

Not for you if…

✗

You're looking for therapy, clinical treatment, or crisis support.

✗

You dislike live sessions and only want self-paced videos.

✗

Recognised qualifications and certificates are not important for you or your organisation.
