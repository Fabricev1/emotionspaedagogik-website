What will be easier after 21 days

Stay grounded in tense conversations with parents, clients, students, or colleagues.

Fit check

Is this for you?

Perfect if…

You carry emotional responsibility for others — as a team lead, client-facing professional, educator, or self-employed expert.
You want a structured, professional method — not therapy or self-help — that works in real situations.
You need tools you can use immediately and explain clearly to others without burning out.

Not if…

You're looking for therapy or deep personal healing rather than professional tools.
You expect a quick "fix" without trying things out in real situations.

Your learning path

Curriculum at a glance

Five focused modules over 21 days. Each builds on the last — from recognising emotional patterns to applying new tools in real work situations.

1

Module 1 — Awareness

Spot emotional signals early, in yourself and others.

2

Module 2 — Regulation

Learn how to stabilise yourself under stress before reacting.

3

Module 3 — Communication

Name emotions and boundaries clearly without escalation.

4

Module 4 — Boundaries

Protect your energy while staying present and professional.

5

Module 5 — Integration

Transfer what you've learned into everyday leadership and work contexts.

What you get

Video lessons

Private workbook

Reflection prompts
